Methodology

Every brief in this archive follows the same two-step check before publication: primary source first (CISA, NVD, or the vendor's own advisory), then independent industry corroboration as a sanity check, never as an override. Anything that traces only to an aggregator or newsletter digest is excluded or explicitly labeled unverified.

Challenges are welcome and expected. Use the "Challenge This Brief" link on any entry, or browse all open and resolved challenges directly.
