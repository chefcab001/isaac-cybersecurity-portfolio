Risk Management Examples

  • Likelihood and impact framing for prioritization
  • Mitigation recommendation tracking in documented action items

Governance Documentation Examples

Governance evidence is organized for traceability from source artifact to public-safe summary, supporting interview discussion of audit preparation, control validation, and reporting discipline.
